About Union Logistics

The Logistics Hub of the Middle East

Established in 2006, Union Logistics is logically located in the heart of Dubai, UAE - with our own dedicated warehouse inside the Dubai Airport Free Zone. We are equipped with all facilities and services required by our valuable customers and partners in and out of Dubai's freight forwarding markets.

With our expansion into Hong Kong, we now bridge the gap between the Middle East and the Far East, offering seamless end-to-end logistics solutions across air, sea, road, and customs services.
